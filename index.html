About Me

I'm Tigo Deken, an engineering product designer who works at the intersection of how things are made and how people use them.

My background in engineering gives me a ground-level understanding of constraints and systems. My focus on design means I never lose sight of the human on the other end.

I care about work that holds up — mechanically, visually, and over time.
