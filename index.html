About me

Built to make
things that last.

I'm Tigo Deken, an Engineering Product Design student based in Amsterdam. My work sits at the intersection of mechanical thinking and design sensibility — where functional constraints and aesthetic decisions aren't in conflict, they're the same conversation.

I'm drawn to objects that earn their existence through how well they work. Whether it's a refillable cap, a coffee machine teardown, or a wooden balance board — I care about the logic inside the thing as much as the surface of it.

"The details aren't the details.
They make the design."
