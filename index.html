About Me

I'm Tigo Deken, 22, studying Engineering Product Design in Amsterdam. I got into this field because I've always been the person who picks things up, turns them over, and wonders how they were made — and whether they could be better.

My work sits at the edge of engineering and design. I like the constraints. A brief that says "sustainable, premium, and manufacturable" is more interesting to me than one with no rules at all.

Outside of school I do garden work, race cars in simulators, and occasionally convince myself I should learn Blender properly.
