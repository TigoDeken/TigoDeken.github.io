About

Tigo Deken,
22, Amsterdam.

I study Engineering Product Design in Amsterdam. My work lives at the intersection of mechanical thinking and design sensibility — things should be well-made and make sense to use.

I got into this field because I've always been the person who picks things up, turns them over, and wonders how they were made — and whether they could be better. I like constraints. A brief that says "sustainable, premium, and manufacturable" is more interesting to me than one with no rules at all.

Outside of school I do garden work, race cars in simulators, and occasionally convince myself I should learn Blender properly.
